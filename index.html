A Memory Safari

Welcome to Snapimals: A Memory Safari! 🌟

Embark on a WILD Adventure:

Game Setup:

  • 🦁 Choose Your Safari Squad:
    • Are you a lone explorer? Opt for "Single Player" or gather friends for a "Multiplayer" extravaganza (2-4 players).
    • 🐾 Name your safari companions!
  • 🧩 Pick Your Memory Challenge:
    • Safari sizes range from 9 to 81 tiles.
  • ⏰ Select Your Safari Style:
    • Roar into action with "Timed" mode or enjoy a leisurely stroll in "Standard" mode.
  • 🔥 Dare to Go Wild:
    • Easy, Medium, or Hard? Choose your difficulty level, but beware—things get WILD in the jungle!


Gameplay:
  • 🎢 Turns and Twists:
    • Single Player: Match tiles for accuracy or beat the clock in Timed Mode.
    • Multiplayer: Score points by matching tiles while racing against time and your co-explorers.
  • 🎮 Controls:
    • Flip tiles during your turn.
    • 🚀 Beware the elusive JOKER tile on odd-numbered safari layouts—play it smart!


Scoring:
  • Single Player Safari Stars:
    • Timed Mode: Race against time for glory.
    • Standard Mode: Score based on accuracy.
  • Multiplayer Mayhem:
    • Timed Mode: Match tiles and race for EXTRA points.
    • Standard Mode: Score based on the number of matched tiles—may the best explorer win!


Game Over:
  • Solo Adventure End:
    • Discover your accuracy and conquering time (if applicable).
    • 🌟 Ready for more? Start a new game or spice it up with different configurations.
  • Multiplayer Showdown:
    • 🤠 Crown the Jungle Champion!
    • 🎉 Ready for a rematch? Start a new game or switch up the challenge.


Wild Fun Extras:
  • 🎶 Immerse yourself in the jungle beats with exciting audio cues.
  • 🃏 Watch out for the JOKER tile—penalties await the daring!
